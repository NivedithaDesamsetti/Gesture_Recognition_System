About the Gesture Recognition System

Our Gesture Recognition System is designed to assist communication for people who are deaf or hard of hearing. By leveraging real-time hand tracking technology, the system accurately detects gestures and converts them into spoken language.

The system uses state-of-the-art machine learning models to recognize sign language gestures. With a simple webcam setup, users can communicate effectively using gestures, which are translated into voice output or text. It’s an intuitive and user-friendly way to bridge communication gaps for the deaf and dumb community.

Explore our platform, learn different signs, train new gestures, and make use of live detection to assist in everyday communication.
